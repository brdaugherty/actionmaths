# Quantum Field Theory: The Substrate of Reality

This visualization moves beyond the "particle" model and illustrates the perspective of **Quantum Field Theory (QFT)**. It demonstrates that reality is not composed of empty space filled with objects, but rather an underlying, vibrating field where particles are merely localized excitations.

## How Probability is Involved

In this demo, probability is the mechanism that determines when and where the underlying field "solidifies" into a measurable event.

### 1. The Zero-Point Field (ZPF)
The background noise you see is the **Zero-Point Field**. According to QFT, even "empty" space is filled with fluctuating energy. This field exists in a state of pure probability, with virtual particles appearing and disappearing constantly.

### 2. The Wave as a Field Excitation
When you trigger an emission, you aren't "firing a bullet." You are creating a **coherent excitation** in the field. This ripple is a "Probability Wave" that changes the energy density of the substrate as it propagates.

### 3. Instantiation (The Logic Gate Collapse)
As the wave expands, it intersects with yellow detectors.
- **The Handshake:** When the wave front touches a detector, a probabilistic "handshake" occurs.
- **The Global Update:** If the lottery is "won," a **Global State Update** is triggered. The wave instantly zeroes out across the entire grid—no matter how large it had become—and a massive energy spike (Instantiation) appears at the detector.

## Theoretical Significance: Emergent Spacetime
Notice the underlying grid warping. This visualizes a key concept in modern physics: **Spacetime is not a fixed stage.**
- When the field is in its "Zero-Point" state, the grid is relatively flat.
- When an excitation occurs, the energy density increases, causing the geometry of the grid to deform.
- This demonstrates the relationship between **Energy (QFT)** and **Geometry (General Relativity)**, showing how the presence of "particles" (field excitations) creates what we perceive as the curvature of spacetime.

## Visualization Indicators
- **System State:** Tells you if the field is in its base fluctuating state, propagating a wave, or undergoing an instantaneous collapse.
- **Collapse Coordinates:** Provides the exact "logical address" in the field where the probability wave was instantiated into a localized event.
